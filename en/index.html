As our customer faced the difficulty that the network was too noisy to be tested, we developed the analyzer to help them. The analyzer is a virtual instrument. The hardware is the computer peripheral developed with STM32F407 and the rest is a desktop app running on computer developed with python.

Out analyzer has higher accuracy and sensitivity than common analyzer, such as Bode100.
